Some things I believe:

  • At any point you can decide what you want to do in your life.
  • The more communities you become part of the more different sides of the world you will see, and ultimately realize that it was always the game of perspectives.
  • Any crazy idea can become big, the only thing that stops you from seeing it in reality is you and the world.
  • Even from bad experiences there are too many learnings.
  • Starting young has no side effects.
  • I used to think that I can control the results based on my effort, but there's always a power that is out of your reach to understand, you can spend your whole life understanding that.
  • People ask how do you know what's good or bad, I believe that there's always an inner voice that's never wrong and you should follow it.
  • Jumping in the situation is the best way to move fast, because fear will make you do stuff that you never imagined.
  • If you surrender now, there's no way you will be able to see the next step.
  • You only need one crazy hope to survive the hardest times of your life, and stay delusional about the future.
  • The world is quite a harsh and transactional place, and there are gonna be days when you are alone with the gods, and you have to survive on your own.
  • The moment you start giving to the world, signals awaken—your inner voice grows clear. While receiving, those same signals stay dormant, blocked by the noise of wanting.
